We are Haraka Solutions Limited

Founded in 2020, HARAKA SOLUTIONS LTD is a leading full-service technology partner delivering end-to-end project strategy, infrastructure installation, construction, and maintenance services for telecom and utility enterprises. Specializing in turnkey solutions across four core domains—telecom infrastructure deployment, power systems engineering, civil design/construction, and technology product distribution—we combine cutting-edge innovation with rigorous quality standards to optimize client outcomes. Our mission: to empower forward-thinking organizations with infrastructure solutions that marry operational excellence with market-proven reliability, partnering exclusively with industry leaders who prioritize sustainable growth through engineering precision. We exist to transform complex challenges into seamless, future-ready systems.

Our Story

Our Story Founded in 2020, Haraka Solutions Ltd emerged from a bold vision: to redefine excellence in Africa’s infrastructure landscape. Born at the intersection of rapid technological advancement and growing demand for reliable utilities, we set out to bridge the gap between visionary engineering and practical execution. From our first project, we’ve been driven by an unwavering commitment to Quality, Reliability, and Innovation – principles that guide every telecom tower we erect, every power grid we optimize, and every smart infrastructure solution we deploy. Over the years, we’ve evolved into a strategic partner for enterprises navigating the complex terrain of:

• Telecom Infrastructure: 5G-ready network deployments, fiber optic installations, and IoT integration

• Power Systems: Sustainable energy solutions and grid modernization

• Civil Engineering: Precision design-build services for smart cities and industrial complexes

• Tech Distribution: Cutting-edge hardware/software partnerships for seamless implementation
